About game

Enemy

The Guardian of the Galaxy is a battle through hordes of space aliens

Levels

The game has only one level with constantly growing complexity

Goal

The main goal is to score as many points as possible

Description

The game test the player's speed and reaction time.
The main goal is to score as many points as you can.
You can kill enemy ships. For each ship you get ten points.
Be careful with aliens. If you touch them, you lose one life.
You can collect barrels. This gives you ten points. If you damage the barrel, you will not get points. If you meet a black hole, it will move you to any of them.
If a bullet hits your ship, you lose one life.
Good Luck!

Controls

Use the arrows to move the ship

Shoot by pressing the Space button
